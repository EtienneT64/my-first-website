Service Dogs

Facts About Dogs

Dogs are able to provide several health benefits. These include lowering stress, increasing fitness and improving happiness. Service dogs, through training, are able to perform specific and beneficial tasks, such as guiding a blind person!

Types Of Service Dogs

Service dogs come in all shapes and sizes! There are guide dogs, which help blind people navigate around, mobility dogs which help injured people to navigate as well. There are also medical alert dogs, who can help alert diabetics with managing their blood sugar level.

Service Dog Breeds

Common service dog breeds for guide dogs include German Shepherds, Labradors and Golden Retrievers. Other service dog breeds include Poodles, Great Danes and many more.
